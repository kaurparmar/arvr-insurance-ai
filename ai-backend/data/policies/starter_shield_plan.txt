Policy Name: Family Fortress Comprehensive Plan
Coverage Details: This policy provides complete health and life insurance protections for up to 4 family members. It covers hospitalization costs up to 500,000 INR per annum, surgical expenses, critical illness coverage, and emergency ambulance services.
Exclusions: Pre-existing conditions are excluded during the initial 24 months of continuous coverage. Cosmetic treatments, dental surgeries unless caused by an accident, and self-inflicted injuries are strictly excluded from coverage.
Claim Process: Claims must be filed within 30 days of hospital discharge along with certified medical reports, original receipts, and identification proof.
